Standard Terminal Arrival Route (STAR)

  • A STAR is a published prcedure and aicraft can take for arrival nearby an airport
  • A STAR explains the flight plan from higher altitude down to the VOR
  • STAR at Wikipedia

STAR's for EGLL London Heathrow

  • All the STAR's in EGLL's case end up at VOR stations
  • The STAR's accomodate approaches from any direction
  • The end of the STAR is the start of the "Final Approach Preocedure"

NOTE: arrival is named vie endpoint, eg BNN4A ends at BNN

  • LAM Lambourne - for E/NE arrivals
  • OCK Ockham - for S/SW arrivals
  • TOMMO TOMMO - for W arrivals
  • BOV Bovingdon - for NW/N arrivals
